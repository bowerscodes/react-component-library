# TextInput

A `<TextInput/>` component is used to capture brief text responses from the 
user.

<Canvas>
  <Story name='Default'>
    <TextInput id='text-input' />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## In a Group

Wrapping a TextInput in a `<Group/>` allows a `<Label>`, as well as an 
optional `<Hint/>` and `<Error/>` to be added.

<Canvas>
  <Story name='In a Group'>
    <Group 
      id='text-input-group'
      label='Please enter your Email address'
      hint='We will never share your email with anyone else.'
      error='Please enter a valid email address'  
    >
      <TextInput id='text-input' error />
    </Group>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Alternative Sizes

The `<TextInput/>` component can be rendered in a variety of sizes by passing 
in a value to the `width` prop. Valid values are `'s'`, `'m'`, `'l'`, `'xl'`, 
`'one-third'`, `'one-half'` and `'two-thirds'`.

  </Story>
