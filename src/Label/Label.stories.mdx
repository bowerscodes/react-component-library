# Label

The `<Label>` component renders its `children` within a `<label>` tag. It 
is most often used to label form inputs. 

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Default">
    <Label id="example">Default Label</Label>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Alternative Sizes

The optional `size` prop can be passed a value of 
`'xs' | 's' | 'm' | 'l' | 'xl'` to adjust the font size.

  <Story name="Alternative Sizes">
    <Label id="example" size="xs">Extra Small Label</Label>
    <Label id="example" size="s"> Small Label</Label>
    <Label id="example" size="m">Medium Label</Label>
    <Label id="example" size="l">Large Label</Label>
    <Label id="example" size="xl">Extra Large Label</Label>
  </Story>
